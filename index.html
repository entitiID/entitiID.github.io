What is Entiti?

The Entiti solution provides a transparent way for individuals and organisations to obtain verify and share identity credentials with one another. It offers opportunities for automation of identity linked processes such as customer registration or KYC (Know Your Customer), access or accessibility to products and services.

Access opportunities

Digital identity can offer less challenging access to credit, banking and other economic opportunities

Empowerment

Women and children can maintain financial independence due to new opportunities from digital Identity

Efficient disbursements

Support from organisations are an “easier sell” when beneficiaries are accessible as a consequence of digital identity
